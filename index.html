Mr.Bean

The man who makes people laugh.

Mr.Bean Life

  • 1956 - Mr.Bean was born in Consett, United Kingdom
  • 1990-1995 - "Mr. Bean" series aired on television
  • 1997 - "Bean: The Ultimate Disaster Movie" was released.
  • 2002 - Another film, "Mr. Bean's Holiday," was released.
  • 2007 - Rowan Atkinson announced that he would be retiring the character of Mr. Bean.
  • 2012 - Rowan Atkinson reprised his role as Mr. Bean in a memorable and comedic performance.
  • 2015 - Rowan Atkinson celebrated the 25th anniversary of Mr. Bean with a special appearance at Buckingham Palace, where he drove onto the forecourt in character.
